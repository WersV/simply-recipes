Banana Pancakes

Indulge in our delightful Banana Pancakes: fluffy, golden treats with a natural sweetness from ripe bananas. Moist, flavorful, and satisfying, they're perfect for breakfast or brunch. Stack, drizzle with maple syrup, and top with sliced bananas for a mouthwatering twist. Whether a banana lover or seeking something new, our pancakes will become a beloved addition to your breakfast repertoire.

Prep Time:

30 min

Cook Time:

15 min

Serving:

4 servings

Tags: Beef Breakfast Pancakes Food

Instructions

STEP 1

Gather your ingredients.

STEP 2

In a mixing bowl, combine the mashed banana, flour, sugar, baking powder, and cinnamon. Mix well.

STEP 3

In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.

STEP 4

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined, avoiding overmixing.

STEP 5

Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Scoop about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.

Ingrediens

1 ripe banana, mashed

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 tablespoon sugar

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

1/2 cup milk

1 egg

1 tablespoon melted butter

Tools

Medium sizen mixing bowl

Whisk

Non-Stick Skillet

Spatula
